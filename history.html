History Of OS

  • The first operating systems to manage tape storage were developed in the late 1950s
  • The General Motors Research Lab developed the first OS for their IBM 701 in the early 1950s
  • In the mid-1960s, operating systems began using the disks
  • The first version of the Unix OS was developed during the late 1960s
  • Microsoft's first Designed OS was DOS. It was founded in 1981, when a Seattle corporation purchased the 86-DOS program
  • First came into existence in 1985, when a GUI was developed and combined with MS-DOS, the current common Windows OS.
